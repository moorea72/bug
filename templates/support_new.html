Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start staking?

Go to the Stake page, select your preferred cryptocurrency and staking plan, enter the amount you want to stake, and confirm your transaction.

When can I withdraw my funds?

You can withdraw your staked funds plus earnings after the staking period completes. Check your active stakes on the Stake page for maturity dates.

How does the referral system work?

Share your referral link with friends. You'll earn commissions when they stake, plus a 20 USDT bonus and premium benefits after 2 successful referrals.

Are my funds safe?

Yes, we use advanced security measures including cold storage, multi-signature wallets, and regular security audits to protect your funds.
